Security

At GeetHub, we take security seriously. Our music player web app is designed with security in mind to ensure your data is safe and your privacy is protected.

Here are some of the security measures we implement:

  • Encrypted connections to protect your data transmission.
  • Regular security audits to identify and fix vulnerabilities.
  • Strict access controls to ensure only authorized users can access sensitive information.
  • Secure storage mechanisms to safeguard your personal data.

If you have any security concerns or would like to report a vulnerability, please contact our security team at abhishekabhinav.av@gmail.com.
