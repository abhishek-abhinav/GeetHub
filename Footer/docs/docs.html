Introduction

Welcome to GeetHub, a responsive music player web app designed to provide you with a seamless music listening experience.

GeetHub offers a variety of features, including:

  • Access to a variety of playlists for different moods and genres
  • Playback control for individual songs ("Geet") and playlists ("GeetList")
  • Seek functionality to jump to specific timestamps in songs
  • Volume control for adjusting audio levels
  • Responsive design ensuring accessibility across all devices

Getting Started

To get started with GeetHub, follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit the GeetHub website or download the app from your app store (not released on Google Play Store/App Store yet) .
  2. Sign up for an account or log in if you already have one (feature coming soon!).
  3. Browse through the available playlists or search for specific songs.
  4. Select a song or playlist to start playing.
  5. Use the playback controls to manage your listening experience.

Usage

To make the most out of GeetHub, follow these guidelines:

  • Browse through the available playlists by clicking on them to expand.
  • Click on individual songs to play them.
  • Use the playback controls to pause, play, skip to the next or previous song, and adjust volume.
  • Utilize the seek bar to jump to specific timestamps within a song.
  • Explore additional features such as shuffle and repeat modes for an enhanced listening experience (not release yet).
